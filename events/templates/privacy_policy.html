Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 04, 2026

GDPR Compliant Data Protection CCPA Ready

Introduction

At ZOZAPRIME, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your data when you use our event management and ticketing platform. We are fully compliant with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), and other applicable data protection laws.

By using ZOZAPRIME, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree with any part of this policy, please do not use our services.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

We collect the following types of personal information when you use our services:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, phone number, username, and password
  • Profile Information: Profile picture, business name, business description, and user preferences
  • Payment Information: Billing address, payment method details (processed securely through M-Pesa and other third-party payment providers)
  • Event Data: Events you create, attend, or show interest in
  • Communication Data: Messages, support requests, feedback, and correspondence with ZOZAPRIME

Automatically Collected Information

We automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our platform:

  • Usage Data: Pages visited, time spent on pages, click patterns, and user interactions
  • Device Information: IP address, browser type, operating system, device identifiers, and screen resolution
  • Location Data: Approximate location based on IP address (with your consent for precise location)
  • Cookies and Tracking: Cookie data and similar tracking technologies (see our Cookie Policy)
  • Analytics Data: Performance metrics, conversion rates, and user behavior patterns

How We Use Your Information

Service Provision

We use your information to:

  • Create and manage your account on ZOZAPRIME
  • Process event registrations and ticket purchases
  • Facilitate M-Pesa and other payment transactions
  • Generate QR code tickets with HMAC encryption for security
  • Provide customer support and respond to your inquiries
  • Send important notifications about your events, purchases, and account
  • Personalize your experience on our platform
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and security incidents

Analytics and Improvement

We use data to:

  • Analyze platform usage and improve our services
  • Understand user preferences and behavior patterns
  • Develop new features and optimize existing functionality
  • Ensure platform security and prevent fraudulent activities
  • Conduct research and statistical analysis
  • Monitor and improve platform performance

Marketing and Communications

With your explicit consent, we may use your information to:

  • Send promotional emails about relevant events and platform features
  • Display personalized event recommendations
  • Share success stories and testimonials (with explicit permission)
  • Conduct market research and user surveys
  • Send newsletters about the creative economy in Kenya

You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time by clicking the "unsubscribe" link in our emails or adjusting your account preferences.

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We Do Not Sell Your Personal Information
ZOZAPRIME does not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for their marketing purposes.

Service Providers

We share information with trusted third-party service providers who help us operate our platform:

  • Payment Processors: M-Pesa (Safaricom), Stripe, PayPal for secure payment processing
  • Communication Services: Email service providers, SMS gateways for notifications
  • Analytics Providers: Google Analytics for usage insights and platform optimization
  • Hosting Services: Cloud infrastructure providers for data storage and platform hosting
  • Security Services: Anti-fraud and security monitoring providers

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your data and use it only for the purposes we specify.

Event Organizers

When you purchase tickets to an event, we share necessary information with the event organizer, including:

  • Your name and contact information
  • Ticket purchase details
  • Attendance confirmation

Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations, court orders, and government requests
  • Protect our rights, property, and safety
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users and the public
  • Enforce our Terms of Service

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the new owners. We will notify you via email and/or prominent notice on our platform before your information is transferred.

International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than Kenya. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international data transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission
  • Adequacy decisions from relevant data protection authorities
  • Technical and organizational security measures
  • Compliance with GDPR requirements for data transfers outside the EEA

Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Information: Retained until you delete your account, plus 90 days for backup recovery
  • Transaction Data: Retained for 7 years for legal, accounting, and tax purposes
  • Analytics Data: Retained for 26 months in anonymized form
  • Marketing Communications: Retained until you unsubscribe or request deletion
  • Support Tickets: Retained for 3 years for quality assurance
  • Payment Records: Retained as required by Kenyan financial regulations

After the retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your personal information in accordance with applicable data protection laws.

Your Rights and Choices

Data Subject Rights

Under GDPR, CCPA, and Kenyan data protection laws, you have the right to:

  • Access: Request a copy of your personal information
  • Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Erasure ("Right to be Forgotten"): Request deletion of your personal information
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to another service in a machine-readable format
  • Objection: Object to processing of your personal information for certain purposes
  • Restriction: Limit processing of your personal information in certain circumstances
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent for processing where consent was the legal basis
  • Lodge a Complaint: File a complaint with your local data protection authority

How to Exercise Your Rights: Email us at privacy@zozaprime.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

Account Controls

You can manage your privacy settings through your account dashboard:

  • Update or delete your profile information
  • Control email preferences and notification settings
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data in JSON or CSV format
  • Manage cookie preferences
  • View your data processing history

Cookie Controls

You can control cookies through:

  • Our cookie consent banner (appears on first visit)
  • Browser settings and preferences
  • Opt-out tools for advertising cookies
  • Your account privacy settings

Data Security

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: SSL/TLS encryption for data in transit; AES-256 encryption for data at rest
  • HMAC QR Codes: Cryptographically secure ticket verification to prevent counterfeiting
  • Access Controls: Role-based access control (RBAC) and multi-factor authentication for staff
  • Regular Audits: Annual security assessments and penetration testing
  • Data Minimization: We collect only necessary information
  • Employee Training: Privacy and security awareness programs for all staff
  • Incident Response: 24/7 security monitoring and breach notification procedures
  • Payment Security: PCI DSS compliance for card payments; M-Pesa security protocols

While we use industry-standard security measures, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security but are committed to protecting your information to the best of our ability.

Children's Privacy

ZOZAPRIME is not intended for children under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 (or 16 in the European Economic Area). If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take immediate steps to delete it.

If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at privacy@zozaprime.com.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our website with a new "Last Updated" date
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Displaying prominent notices on our platform
  • Requiring re-acceptance for significant changes

Your continued use of ZOZAPRIME after we make changes constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.
